How do I shop with I'll Pay? It's easy...
  • Install I'll Pay on Google Chrome
  • Install I'll Pay on Mozilla Firefox
  • Install I'll Pay on Safari
  • Install the button in your browser's bookmark tab
  • Go to your favourite online store
  • Find an item you want to purchase
  • Click on the I'll Pay button you installed
  • Make your offer

HOW IT WORKS
FOR SHOPPERS

  1. Drag this button to your bookmarks bar.

  2. Go shopping online at your favorite stores.

  3. Find a product that you want to buy.

  4. Instead of adding to cart - make an offer.

  5. Click the I'llPay button to make an offer.

HOW IT WORKS
FOR RETAILERS

  1. Stuck with old stock? Want another way to generate cashflow?

  2. Create your Account.

  3. Receive Offers.

  4. Choose the Offers you want.

  5. Get more Sales.
